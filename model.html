Development Process

I used blender to create my model. Blender is a 3D modeling computer software which allows you to create 3D models. In Blender I used a variety of skills such as duplicating, scaling and joining to help develop my model. I used the extrude and duplicate tools to make the cylindrical shapes on the sides of my model. I started with a cylinder and then extruded the face up and across. I then repeated this until I had one, and then I duplicated it 4 times. I used the duplication and joining tools to make the chequred design for the floor. I first created two squares and joined them together. I then duplicated these 2 blocks to create another 2 blocks, then I joined these 4 blocks together to make a square. I then repeated this process until the floor was done.
