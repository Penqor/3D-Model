Tilt Brush

For a second 3D design software I decided to use Google Tilt Brush. Tilt Brush is a 3D virtual and interactive painting app. To use Tilt Brush a virtual reality set is required. I used the HTC Vive to use Tilt Brush. In Tilt Brush I tried to recreate the 3D model I made on Blender. It was difficult to do in Tilt Brush as I wan't very accurate with the lines and shapes that I tried to draw. As with Blender I was able accuratley size and postion the shapes I wanted on my model.
