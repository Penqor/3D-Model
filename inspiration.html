Model Inspiration

My model was inspired by multiple things. I wanted my model to look like something out of a gardern. Like a sculpture or tree. The bonsai tree was a big inspiration for my model as I wanted it to appear like a tree by using the cylinders that bend in and out like trunks or branches of a tree much like the bonsai tree.
